AI Agent · 2026

HR Automation AI Agent

An end-to-end HR process automation agent that eliminates repetitive manual work across the entire employee lifecycle — from job posting to onboarding.

The Problem

HR teams were spending 15+ hours/week on repetitive tasks: screening CVs, scheduling interviews, sending follow-up emails, generating offer letters, and answering the same FAQ queries from candidates. These tasks required no real intelligence — just time.

The Solution

A LangChain-powered AI agent with access to tools for: CV parsing and scoring against job criteria, automated calendar scheduling via API, personalised email generation (GPT-4o), document template filling for offer letters, and a RAG-based FAQ bot for candidate queries — all orchestrated in an n8n workflow.

Results

✅ 15+ hours/week of manual HR work eliminated.
✅ Candidate response time reduced from 3 days to under 2 hours.
✅ Offer letter generation time: 3 minutes → 10 seconds.
✅ 95% of candidate FAQs handled without human involvement.

AI Data Analyst Automation

An autonomous AI analyst that connects to any dataset, generates insights, builds charts, and delivers polished executive-ready reports — without a human analyst required.

The Problem

Business teams were waiting 3–5 days for analysts to produce reports from raw data. The process was manual, repetitive, and a bottleneck for every decision-making cycle.

Alert System · 2026

Real-Time Stock & Expiry Alerts for Medical Shops

An automated real-time monitoring system that tracks medicine inventory levels and expiry dates, firing instant WhatsApp/SMS alerts before stock runs out or medicines expire.

The Problem

Medical shop owners were regularly losing money due to: medicines expiring unnoticed, running out of critical stock without warning, and manual stock checks consuming 2+ hours daily. Expired medicines also posed a serious compliance risk.

The Solution

A Python scheduler connected to the shop's inventory spreadsheet (or simple database) that runs checks every morning: flags medicines expiring within 30 days, alerts on stock below threshold quantities, and sends categorised WhatsApp messages to the owner via the WhatsApp Business API — all automated, zero manual checking.

Results

✅ Zero expired medicines dispatched since deployment.
✅ Stock-out incidents eliminated for monitored items.
✅ 2 hours/day of manual stock checking saved.
✅ Owner receives a morning summary every day automatically.
